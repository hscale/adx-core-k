# ADX CORE - Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Platform Subscriptions (Primary Revenue)

#### Starter Plan: $99/month
- Up to 10 users
- Basic multi-tenancy
- 10GB file storage
- **Powerful workflow orchestration with rule-based automation**
- Email support
- **Target**: Small businesses, startups

#### Professional Plan: $299/month
- Up to 50 users
- Advanced authentication (SSO, MFA)
- 100GB file storage
- Client management plugin included
- **AI-enhanced workflows with intelligent planning and optimization**
- Priority support
- **Target**: Growing companies, agencies

#### Enterprise Plan: $999/month
- Unlimited users
- White-label customization
- Unlimited file storage
- All default plugins included
- **Custom AI models and advanced workflow intelligence**
- Dedicated support
- On-premise deployment option
- **Target**: Large enterprises, resellers

### 2. Plugin Marketplace (Secondary Revenue)

#### Revenue Sharing Model
- **Free Plugins**: 0% commission (community building)
- **Paid Plugins**: 30% commission to ADX CORE, 70% to developer
- **Enterprise Plugins**: 20% commission (volume incentive)

#### Plugin Categories
- **AI Intelligence**: Workflow optimization, predictive analytics, intelligent automation
- **Business Management**: CRM, Project Management, Invoicing
- **Integrations**: Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Teams
- **Analytics**: Advanced reporting, BI dashboards
- **Security**: Advanced auth, compliance tools
- **Industry-Specific**: Healthcare, Legal, Real Estate

#### AI Plugin Ecosystem Strategy
- **Core Platform**: Provides robust workflow orchestration for all users
- **AI Plugins**: Optional intelligence layer for premium customers
- **Tiered Access**: Basic (rule-based) → Premium (AI-enhanced) → Enterprise (custom AI)
- **Revenue Model**: AI plugins command premium pricing due to advanced capabilities
- **Market Differentiation**: Hybrid approach allows competitive pricing while offering AI upsells

#### Projected Plugin Revenue
- **Year 1**: $100K (marketplace launch)
- **Year 2**: $2M (ecosystem growth)
- **Year 3**: $10M (mature marketplace)

### 3. Professional Services (Tertiary Revenue)

#### Custom Development
- **Plugin Development**: $50K-200K per plugin
- **Custom Integrations**: $25K-100K per integration
- **White-Label Setup**: $10K-50K per deployment

#### Consulting Services
- **Implementation**: $150/hour
- **Training**: $200/hour
- **Architecture Review**: $250/hour

#### Support Services
- **Premium Support**: $500/month per customer
- **Dedicated Success Manager**: $2K/month per customer
- **24/7 Support**: $1K/month per customer

## Pricing Strategy

### Value-Based Pricing
- **Price based on business value delivered**
- **Not per-user pricing** (encourages adoption)
- **Feature-based tiers** (clear upgrade path)

### Competitive Positioning
- **Premium vs. WordPress**: Higher price, enterprise features
- **Competitive vs. Salesforce**: Lower price, better performance
- **Value vs. Custom Development**: Faster, proven, extensible

### Pricing Psychology
- **Anchor High**: Enterprise plan sets value perception
- **Sweet Spot**: Professional plan designed for majority
- **Entry Point**: Starter plan removes barriers

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Product-Led Growth (Months 1-12)
- **Free Trial**: 30-day full access
- **Freemium Model**: Limited free tier
- **Self-Service**: Easy signup and onboarding
- **Viral Features**: Team invitations, client portals

### Phase 2: Sales-Led Growth (Months 13-24)
- **Inside Sales**: Professional plan focus
- **Enterprise Sales**: Dedicated enterprise team
- **Channel Partners**: Agencies and consultants
- **Customer Success**: Reduce churn, drive expansion

### Phase 3: Ecosystem-Led Growth (Months 25+)
- **Plugin Marketplace**: Developer ecosystem
- **Partner Program**: Integration partners
- **Community**: User conferences, forums
- **Platform Strategy**: Become industry standard

## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risks
- **Competition**: Focus on differentiation and speed
- **Economic Downturn**: Flexible pricing, cost management
- **Technology Shifts**: Modular architecture, continuous innovation

### Operational Risks
- **Talent Acquisition**: Competitive compensation, remote-first
- **Scaling Challenges**: Proven technologies, gradual growth
- **Security Breaches**: Security-first design, compliance focus
